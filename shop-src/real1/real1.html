Baby Diapers FAQs
1. Can we use a diaper for a newborn baby?
Yes, you can use a diaper for your newborn baby, but keep a few things in mind. Choose a diaper made with soft and absorbent material to keep your baby's skin hygienic and dry. You can also opt for newborn diapers which are specially designed with the correct sizing and materials kept in mind.
2. Are diapers good for babies?
Diapers are convenient and keep your baby's skin dry and soft. However, ensure that you change your baby’s diapers often, use a good moisturiser or oil, and choose a good quality diaper brand.
3. Do diapers cause a rash?
Diaper rash is usually caused due to excessive dryness or friction. A good way to prevent it is by applying a good moisturiser, changing the diaper frequently and buying the right-sized diapers made of soft material.
4. How long can a baby wear a diaper at night?
A highly absorbent diaper can stay put for 10-12 hours, especially if it is specifically designed for night-time wear. However, if your baby’s skin does not react well to long-term diaper wear, you should increase the frequency of diaper changes.
5. How to determine what diaper size a baby needs?
Diaper size is determined by the age and weight of the baby. You can check the age and weight range mentioned on the diaper packaging to choose the best size for your baby. Here is a sample sizing chart based on weight. 4-8 kgs: Small7-12 kgs: Medium9-14 kgs: Large12-17 kgs: Extra Large
6. How do you know when a diaper is wet?
Many diapers have a wetness indicator that signals when the baby needs a diaper change. However, if the diapers you use for your baby does not have a wetness indicator, you can find out through other methods. Hold the front section of the diaper and jiggle it. If it moves easily or in a jelly-like way, it requires changing. Your baby may also cry in discomfort due to wetness, signaling the need for a diaper change.
7. Which are the best baby diapers for a newborn?
Some of the best diaper brands in India for babies are Huggies, Pampers, Himalaya, Babyhug and Mamypoko, among others.
8. Do diapers expire?
While diapers do not technically have an expiration date, most manufacturing companies recommend using them within 2 years of purchasing for the best absorbency, elasticity and tackiness.
